THE LARGEST ASIAN SEAFOOD RESTAURANT IN MELBOURNE

Our seafood restaurant is a restaurant that specializes in seafood cuisine and seafood dishes, such as fish and shellfish. Dishes may include freshwater fish. Our seafood restaurants also provide retail sales of seafood that consumers take home to prepare. Our restaurants have a marine-themed decor, with decorations such as fish nets, nautical images and buoys. Also our restaurants offer additional non-seafood items, such as chicken and beef dishes.
